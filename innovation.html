ABHIGYAN-17 MUTBI Visit World Enterpreneur's Day

The event provided a platform for students to exhibit their projects thereby, encouraging and supporting them to nurture and enhance their potential in lieu with their academic and industrial pursuit. . It was a driving force created to nurture the innovation in every student’s mind. A pioneering effort conceived by creative minds, it was a splendid adventure within itself.

University Industry Interaction Center with an objective of to Gain the knowledge about Incubation Center, visited Manipal University Technological Business Incubator (MUTBI) on 17/06/2019. We met Ms. Asmitha Asst. Manager MUTBI and Dr. Radhakrishna, CEO, MUTBI and understood their working model. Felt happy that we are in far with them.

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell of UIIC organized Panel session on “Leveraging campus resources and equipping oneself with key entrepreneurial skills during your higher education”to mark World Entrepreneur’s Day on 21st Aug 2018.
